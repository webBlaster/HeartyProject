ABOUT THE HEARTY PROJECT

We have a long story on our journey up to this point, but here is a fair summary

The Hearty Project came from a desire to harness the power of recent technology for advancing medicine; its an ongoing effort that has been contributed too by a number of people over the past few years and now based on learning,insights,we're on a few technologies that can be easily exploited and an area of care that we consider most receptive i.e. Cardiovascular Preventive Care.

Simply put, a system for better managing hypertension through improved monitoring Over the next year, we'll optimize for the best solution that creates value with our pioneers, conduct a study to verify if the solution helps improve compliance + blood pressure decline; and finally we'll scale the use to increase the amount of data to such as is necessary to build useful predictive models or other valuable insights for care.
